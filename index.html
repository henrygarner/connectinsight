Startups & Investors

If you run a start up or invest in them you will be only too aware of the risks. 3 in 4 startups fail. Most fail because the business becomes product focused and the market opportunity isn’t validated.

Those that do grow explore the opportunity for their product by analysing competitors and alternatives and use this information in their commercial and product strategy.

This doesn't hinder the fast pace of a startup as output is clear and actionable. You will be confident in creating strategies based on knowledge rather than assumption, turning your startup into a growth business.

Speak to us about:

  • Market due diligence
  • Product validation
  • Market positioning strategy
  • Building a commercial strategy
  • Executing a marketing strategy

About Connect Insight

Connect Insight was founded by Helen Gawor. Helen has been at the forefront of digital marketing and media strategy for over 12 years. Helen was Head of Commercial Strategy at Orange when she left the company in 2006, after growing the division for more than 6 years (previously branded as Freeserve then Wanadoo). She was responsible for media research, trade marketing and PR, and sales strategy.

Helen has been nominated for 6 industry awards for ground breaking digital consumer insight and won a Campaign Media Award for Best Research for her work into how online ad formats appeal to different audiences. At Orange she lead many relevant industry committees, and as a consultant has worked as an advisor to the World Federation of Advertisers on young people and digital issues.

As a Digital Strategy Consultant, Helen has taken her mantra of making insight accessible, contributing to strategy and revenue generation to her new clients. Connect Insight’s clients have included Jaguar, Debenhams and American Express, as well as a number of creative and brand agencies, publishers/media businesses and social media consultancies. She is also currently working at board level with innovative digital start-ups and their investors.
